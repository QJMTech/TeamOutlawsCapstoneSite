Our Client

Dr. Doerry

Dr. Doerry, our client, is a computer science teacher at Northern Arizona University, as well as the director of the CS Capstone class. Having lead the capstone experience for over 15 years, he has found innumerable instances where some of the more mundane tasks could be streamlined. Enter TeamBandit Teams Management Portal.

Our Mission

Small agile teams are quickly becoming a cornerstone of the tech industry as well as many others. Managing and maintaining dozens of these teams can quickly become overwhelming for any one person. Our client, Dr. Doerry, is looking for a way to streamline this process to reduce the time spent setting up and monitoring dozens of projects concurrently. TeamBandit is to serve as a portal where all of the different functions required of this monumental task can be centralized and easily accessed.
