TeamBandit

TeamBandit is a powerful web application designed to facilitate the running of team-based classes. It accomplishes this by eliminating many of the inefficiencies these courses present through helpful modules. Both students and organizers access the application through the same route, being directed to their respective view after our backend decides which role they have. Once they are signed in, data that corresponds to them is pulled from the database and displayed. This can be anything from courses to emails.

Improved Workflow

TeamBandit's improved workflow seeks to simplify tasks for both students and organizers, condensing the spread out nature of managing and participating in these courses. Some key parts of this include:

  • Course Management
  • Client Management
  • Project Preferences
