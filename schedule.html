Our Philosophy

TeamBandit’s development cycle has taken place over the course of the last few months and has utilized a continuous integration approach in order to get the product in user’s hands as soon as possible. Development began in December with the creation and formatting of our AWS server, which is where our application is hosted. As shown, each component is allotted a relatively short amount of time due to our minimal focus on the aesthetics of the project in the early phases of development, as we believe accomplishing a finished project by the time the semester ends is our primary concern.
